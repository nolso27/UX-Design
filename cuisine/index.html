Local Food Specialties

When visiting Taniti, be sure to try these local specialties that showcase the island's unique culinary heritage:

  • Coconut Fish Curry - Fresh fish simmered in coconut milk with local spices and served with island-grown rice.
  • Taro Root Pudding - A sweet dessert made from locally grown taro root, coconut milk, and island honey.
  • Grilled Fish Skewers - Fresh fish marinated in citrus and herbs, skewered and grilled over open flames.
  • Tropical Fruit Platter - A selection of island-grown fruits including passion fruit, mango, papaya, and star fruit.
  • Volcanic Rice - A specialty dish where rice is cooked with black volcanic salt and local herbs.

Many restaurants offer special "island night" events featuring traditional music, dance performances, and a buffet of local specialties. Ask your hotel concierge for recommendations and reservation assistance.
